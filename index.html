How it works

Select your game design

In this first step we will start designing the game and thinking about how to make
your idea a fun game. If you choose to make puzzle games, for example, we are going to study
what makes these games good.

Get a prototype

At this stage, we will present a prototype of the game you have chosen,
so that you will know if it agrees with your ideas.

Wait for the production

After we present the prototype of the game, we will produce it.
Build all the mechanics, graphics, fix bugs and get the game ready for you.

Get your game

After completion, you will receive your game ready and you can use it the
way you want, feel free to publish it on the platform you want.
